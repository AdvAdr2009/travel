INDIA

India is located in Southern Asia and is a unique country. It is the largest democracy in the world. It is the 7th largest country and is the 2nd highest populated country in the world. The capital of India is New Delhi. The most populous city and the commercial capital is Mumbai. It has a variety of cultures and has different types of citizens. India has 22 official languages. They are Hindi, English, Malayalam, Tamil, Assamese, Oriya, Maratha, Telugu, Urdu, Bengali, Gujarati, Nepali, Manipuri, Kannada, Sindhi, Dogri, Santhali, Sanskrit, Maithili, Bodo, Punjabi and Kashmiri. The climate climate and landscape also varies between different areas. Central India is home to the Deccan Plateau, the western coast extends from Gujarat to Kanyakumari and the eastern coast extends from Kanyakumari to Kolkata.

India has many important and beautiful tourist spots. They are, the Taj Mahal, the Red Fort, The Jantar Mantars, the Tomb of Humayun, the Golden Temple of Amritsar, the Gateway of India, India Gate and Victoria Memorial. The Periyar Wildlife National Park, Munnar, Ooty, Kovalam Beach, Kumarakom, Hampi, Kanyakumari etc. are all located in South India.

Indian food also varies when you travel between different states. In North India, you might get served an Aloo Paratha. When you travel to Kerala and hear that their main item is "Porotta and Beef Roast", you will get confused when you eat it. Sambar and Dosa is popular for Tamil Nadu, but just north of Andhra Pradesh, in Odisha, the staple food is Pakhala Bhata Made of cooked rice soaked in curd and water served with fried fish, potatoes or Badi choora with papad on the side. Some food items also have different names in different places. When Panipuri is named so in Western India, it is named 'Gupchup' in Odisha, 'Puchka' in Bengal and 'Golgappa' in the north.

Fun Fact: India has its own tectonic plate seperate from Asia shared with other countries such as Sri Lanka, Pakistan and Bangladesh. There is rumour that Australia is also included in this tectonic plate and that it is a large Indo-Australian tectonic plate.

CHINA

China is located in East Asia and mostly consists of mountains and plateaus. But there are 2 deserts - The Taklamakan Desert and the Gobi Desert. It is the most populous country in the world. The capital of China is Beijing. The most populous city is Shanghai.

The most famous structure in China is the Great Wall of China. It was built during the reigns of different dynasties over 2000 years from 7th century BCE durind the Chu state till 1878 in the Ming dynasty rule. Most of the remains we see today were built by the Ming Dynasty to protect themselves from the invaders of the north and the west. Another famous tourist destination is the Forbidden City. It was the palace of the Chinese rulers in Beijing. Any of the citizens were forbidden to enter the palace, thus the name came to be. The Forbidden City is now a museum. The temple of heaven is another destination. The Chinese rulers went there regularly to pray for a good harvest. The Shanghai Tower is a 127-story high rise tower with 2 observatories. A disneyland amusement park is also located in Shanghai - The Shanghai Disneyland Park.

CENTRAL ASIA

Central Asia is a subregion of Asia, which stretches from the Caspian Sea in the west to China and Mongolia in the east, and from Afghanistan and Iran in the south to Russia in the north. The region consists of the former Soviet republics of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan.

The historical cities of Usbekistan - Samarkhand, Bukhara and Khiva are important trade centres. They were located on the ancient Silk Road and were prosperous cities. Central Asia is filled with many lakes - Song Kul is a very beautiful and lush alpine lake surrounded by summer pasture, Issyk Kul is mountain lake in Kyrgystan, and there are beautiful turqoise lakes in Tajikistan's Fann Mountains. The "Darvaza", located in Turkmenistan, is a gas crater also known as, "The Gate of Hell".
